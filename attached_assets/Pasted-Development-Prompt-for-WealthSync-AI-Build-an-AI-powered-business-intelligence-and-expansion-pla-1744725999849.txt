Development Prompt for WealthSync AI:

Build an AI-powered business intelligence and expansion platform called WealthSync AI, designed for both individual users and companies, with two tiers: Normal (Basic) and Premium (Subscription Plus). The platform must offer an intuitive dashboard with real-time AI assistance, global/local insights, interactive visualization tools, and automation features.

👤 User Types & Subscriptions
Include two main account types: Individual Users and Companies.

For each, enable two subscription levels: Normal (Basic) and Premium (Subscription Plus).

Ensure clear onboarding flows that adapt based on user type and subscription level.

🔹 Features for Normal (Basic) Subscribers
🧍 Individual Users:
Allow limited business matchmaking, connecting users to up to 3 relevant partners or investors per month using AI filters based on goals, region, and sector.

Integrate a smart AI chatbot assistant to answer business questions, guide through compliance, and offer growth tips.

Display basic country insights such as market size, current growth rate, and general startup climate.

Provide basic access to funding and grant discovery, showing limited but relevant opportunities based on profile.

Enable AI-powered task assistant to help with simple reminders, schedules, and task summaries.

Include a basic interactive business intelligence map:

Let users hover and click on countries to view key industry highlights.

Show startup activity levels and investment hubs using colored markers.

Offer read-only access to a knowledge graph, allowing exploration of relationships between industries, companies, and sectors.

Provide access to 3 downloadable legal templates such as NDA, partnership agreement, or founder agreement.

Limit AI credit usage for tasks, chat, document generation, and map interactions.

🏢 Companies:
Allow companies to create a profile including sector, size, market, and goals.

Display a basic market potential report that outlines estimated opportunity sizes by location.

Include a compliance checklist per selected country or region.

Provide access to the interactive map (basic company view) showing partner zones, regulatory areas, and competitor density.

🔸 Features for Premium (Subscription Plus) Subscribers
🧍 Individual Users:
Enable unlimited business matchmaking with AI-filtered recommendations for strategic partnerships, investors, accelerators, and service providers.

Provide real-time market sentiment analysis by analyzing news, social media, and economic indicators using NLP to detect trends and risks.

Generate a personalized growth roadmap that outlines step-by-step expansion strategies tailored to industry, country, and stage.

Integrate automated legal guidance and document generation, including contract templates adapted by region and business type.

Enable real-time grant and funding alerts tailored to the user’s sector and country, with weekly AI-curated recommendations.

Add AI-powered risk assessments with data visualization tools predicting ROI, volatility, and potential pitfalls.

Include a smart assistant bot for task automation: auto-scheduling meetings, summarizing PDFs, responding to emails.

Offer multi-language AI assistant with support for English, Arabic, Mandarin, French, Spanish, and German.

Allow users to build a custom knowledge graph of their business ecosystem and download it for reports or pitch decks.

Upgrade to a premium interactive map with:

Dynamic heatmap overlays for funding, investment activity, startup density, and policy friendliness.

Filters by sector, region, funding stage, tax zones, and regulations.

Real-time clickable layers revealing partnerships, major players, legal climate, and upcoming events.

Drill-down capability from continent to city level with live AI insights.

🏢 Companies:
Provide a multi-user dashboard for teams to manage business intelligence, chat with AI, assign tasks, and monitor activity.

Integrate AI-generated smart contracts secured via blockchain with auto-filling capabilities.

Offer a market expansion AI advisor suggesting best cities or countries to expand based on competition, funding, and demand.

Allow companies to build and export stakeholder knowledge graphs, mapping out clients, suppliers, competitors, and partners.

Enable premium interactive map tools including:

Enterprise filters for regulations, tax zones, and startup clusters.

Competitive overlays to identify white-space or over-saturated areas.

Ability to annotate, export, and integrate map data into reports and dashboards.

Include an Investor CRM module where the AI tracks outreach, recommends next steps, and manages follow-ups with personalized messaging.

🌍 Map Functionality Summary
Design the Interactive Business Intelligence Map as a dynamic, real-time visual interface where users can explore business environments globally. It must include:

Geographic data layers visualized via heatmaps and tooltips.

Clickable countries and regions that expand into cards with metrics (e.g., GDP growth, investment hotspots, business ease score).

Industry-specific overlays showing sectors with the highest potential, filtered by user interest.

Support for drill-down from global view to regional zones, cities, and even business parks.

Add animated data changes (e.g., rising sectors, cooling investment) based on sentiment and funding activity.

Allow companies to customize views and export map-based insights into reports.

💡 Key Integrations
Use OpenAI or similar LLM for business insights, task assistance, legal drafting.

Integrate funding APIs or public datasets for grant discovery.

Include Stripe or PayTabs integration for payment and subscription management.

Offer map data via Mapbox, Leaflet, or custom D3.js visualizations.

Use Neo4j or a graph database for knowledge graph interactions.
